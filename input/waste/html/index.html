Frequently Asked Questions relating to properties

“Questions about Property Pricing, Market trend, Property Documents and property related services”

How do I Sell/Rent my properties quickly for a better price?
Each property is unique in nature which requires a thoughtful Marketing Strategy to find the right customer for the right price. Focused marketing efforts needed to maximize the market reach along with an intensive follow-up and follow through with the right prospects enables reduction in the turnaround time for property sale or rent. PE-Realtors provide a customized solution to Sell/Rent your property [New or old] through “Priority Marketing Services”.
How do I know my property’s current Market Price?
Property market price depends on various factors such as property location, unique selling proposition, property title, location advantage and future growth. Guidance Value defined by the local authority can be a reference point to assess the fair market value of your property. PE- Realtors provide property valuation and market assessment services.
How do I know if my property document is complete and up to date?
Property documentation should be up to date for your property. It is always a best practice to check the list of documents regularly and meet any latest Government/local authority requirements. PE-Realtors provide “Property Document Verification” services to check the title flow, provide “Legal Opinion” if needed.
Who can assist in my property related legal services?
The title of the property and the documentation play a vital role in deciding the value of the property. Legal assistance with title scrutiny and proper documentation is a must. A lawyer is the best person to guide and correct the documentation. Always keep your property documents updated. Consult a Lawyer and correct the title. PE-Realtors have a legal team who can assist you.
What should I do to post property registration?
From legal point of view property registration [Sale dee Registration] only a partial completion of clear title transfer to your name. There are various other process post registration to be completed to comply with the local authorities requirement. It includes Khath Creation / Transfer, change in the Property Tax system, Change in Electricity connection etc. The set of activities post registration varies from property to property. PE-Realtors provide an end-to-end service to the customer post registration.
